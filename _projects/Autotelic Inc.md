# Brief

Autotelic Inc is a research lab building AI agents that maintain persistent memory across interactions, retaining context, preferences, and project history so they can evolve alongside their users. We developed the brand identity and website for Autotelic, giving visual form to their forward-looking work in artificial intelligence.
