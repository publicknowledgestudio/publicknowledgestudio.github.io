# Brief

SCC Online is one of India's most widely used legal research databases, housing millions of documents spanning case law, statutory material, and international legal resources. Published by Eastern Book Company, it is relied upon daily by advocates, judges, and academic institutions across the country. We provided branding and product design for SCC Online, helping refine the digital experience of the platform.
