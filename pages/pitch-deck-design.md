## Your pitch deck is your first product

Before investors try your product, they see your deck. It's the first experience they have with your company — and it shapes whether they take the next meeting or move on.

A great pitch deck isn't about flashy animations or trendy templates. It's about clarity: a compelling narrative, sharp visuals, and a structure that builds confidence in your team and opportunity.

At Public Knowledge, we help startups design pitch decks that do exactly this.

## What makes a great deck

<div class="landing-cards">
    <div class="landing-card">
        <i class="ph-fill ph-target landing-card-icon"></i>
        <h3>Problem Framing</h3>
        <p>The problem slide should make the audience feel the pain — a clear, visceral statement, not a paragraph of text.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="landing-card">
        <i class="ph-fill ph-path landing-card-icon"></i>
        <h3>Narrative Arc</h3>
        <p>The deck has rhythm. Each section flows into the next. The audience is carried through the story, not dropped into a pile of slides.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="landing-card">
        <i class="ph-fill ph-chart-line-up landing-card-icon"></i>
        <h3>Data Visualisation</h3>
        <p>A well-designed chart is worth more than a bullet point. We make the numbers tell the story.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="landing-card">
        <i class="ph-fill ph-frame-corners landing-card-icon"></i>
        <h3>Restraint</h3>
        <p>White space is not wasted space. Dense slides signal poor prioritisation. We give the audience room to breathe.</p>
