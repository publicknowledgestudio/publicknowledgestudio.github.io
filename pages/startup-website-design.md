## Your website is your hardest-working salesperson

For most startups, the website is the first real touchpoint with users, investors, and partners. It needs to communicate what you do, why it matters, and what to do next — all within seconds.

Template websites get you live fast. But they also make you look like every other startup. When you're competing for attention in a crowded market, your website should feel like *you* — distinctive, intentional, and built for conversion.

At Public Knowledge, we design startup websites from scratch. No templates, no generic UI kits, no shortcuts.

## Why custom over templates

Templates optimise for speed. Custom design optimises for *your* specific story, audience, and conversion goals. When every startup in your space uses the same Framer template, a custom website becomes a genuine competitive advantage.

We also build with SEO in mind from day one — semantic HTML, fast load times, proper meta tags, and content structured for search visibility.
