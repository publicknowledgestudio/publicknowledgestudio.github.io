## Why brand identity matters for startups

Most startups treat branding as an afterthought — something to "figure out later" once the product works. But the startups that break through early tend to have one thing in common: a clear, distinctive identity from day one.

Your brand is the first thing investors see in a pitch deck. It shapes how users perceive your product before they ever try it. And it determines whether people remember you in a market full of noise.

At Public Knowledge, we help startups get this right — early, intentionally, and without the bloated timelines of traditional agencies.
