Mohit is a motion designer and visual storyteller who approaches design through clarity, rhythm, and emotion. Working across motion, product visuals, and emerging interactive tools, he brings a balance of artistic instinct and structured thinking to his work.

A self-taught designer with a foundation in tools like After Effects and DaVinci Resolve, he is continuously expanding his practice through platforms like Rive and Cavalry, exploring new ways motion can respond, adapt, and engage.

With experience spanning both professional work and years of independent exploration, he is drawn to projects that simplify complexity and make ideas instantly understandable while still leaving a lasting emotional impact. His work is rooted in finding the most direct, effective way to visualize a concept, where every frame serves a purpose.
